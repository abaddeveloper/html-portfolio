The Best Films According to Mary

My Top 3 movies of all-time!

Monty Python and the Holy Grail (1975)

This is my favourite Monty Python film. My husband and I still quote it. It has some of Monty Python's best lines ever! It's a must see!

The Matrix (1999)

This Sci-Fi film is one of my all time favourites. It has everything and launched a new genre of cinemetrography that still impacts many of the new films being made today.

The Count of Monte Cristo (2002)

I loved the book and this adaptation starring Jim Cadviezel is my all time favourite It has stunning cinemetrography, brilliant humour and plot lines. A must see!
